# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

This is a community space for SFT-ISU students and alumni. We pledge to keep it welcoming, respectful, and helpful for everyone.

## Expected Behaviour

- Be kind and respectful to fellow students
- Give constructive feedback on PRs
- Report broken links, don't just complain about them
- Don't submit someone else's portfolio without their consent

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Submitting fake or spam portfolios
- Deliberately breaking or vandalising the README

## Enforcement

Violations may result in your PR being closed and/or being blocked from the repository. Reach out to a maintainer if you witness any issues.
